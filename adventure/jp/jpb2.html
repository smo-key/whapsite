The Mongolian Invasion

Japan

You decide to wait for the Mongols to come to you, knowing that defending is easier than attacking.


Over the next few weeks however, the samurai become less and less satisfied just standing by idly while the Mongols could be conquering countryside. In the middle of one night, the Mongols launch a raid. They light fires and spread chaos throughout your camp. In the morning, before you can assess the damage, you see a huge army marching towards the fortress. Before you have time to even form ranks, the Mongols began to fling rocks and dead bodies over the walls. They come in with a battering ram and after knocking through the gates, spill into the fort.

Your unorganized army quickly succumbs to the Mongols and you are killed in action. You die and Japan falls to the Mongol conquerors.
