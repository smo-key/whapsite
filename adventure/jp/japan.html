The Mongolian Invasion

Japan

The year is 1281 CE.

Kublai Khan, grandson of Genghis Khan, is preparing his mighty Mongol navy for the second invasion of Japan.

The Kamakura shogunate refuses to stand idle and mobilizes their defenses. Japan repelled the Mongols once and they can repel them again.

The day of the invasion has arrived. You will lead your nation to either victory or defeat.

Will you prevail?
