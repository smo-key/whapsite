The Mongolian Invasion

Japan

You are the renowned regent of the Kamakura shogunate Hojo Tokimune, feared and respected throughout Japan.


Though by all rights the shogun himself or even the emperor should wield the power you hold, their weakness allowed you to achieve more or less total control of Japan. Now you rule Japan with an iron fist, lording over the lesser lords and samurai.
You were born into a noble family, the son of the previous regent. All your youth you were regaled with tales of the great deeds you would do, the fame you would win as ruler. And you have lived up to these stories. You rose quickly to power, defeating every challenger with the same vigor.
