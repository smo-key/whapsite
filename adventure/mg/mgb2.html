The Mongolian Invasion

Mongolia

You decide to try and conquer as much of Kyushu as possible before the winter.

With the beachhead held, you quickly take over the nearby cities. By October, you have seized control of Fukouka and most of Kyushu, and your reinforcements are finally arriving. Utilizing these new men, you capture all of Kyushu. His greatness Kublai Khan is very impressed with your achievements. He wants to control over all of Japan, and he appoints you commander of the total invasion of Japan. You know that the Japanese will work on their defences throughout the winter, and that it conquering Honshu would be much more difficult if the Japanese are ready. However, though attacking early might win you some land, the Japanese might be able to destroy your army if you don’t wait for reinforcements. But establishing a beachhead or another fort on the coast of Honshu would be immensely helpful to the cause.

What do you do? Do you hold down on Kyushu for the winter? Or do you try to conquer parts of Honshu in hopes of conquering all of Japan sooner?
