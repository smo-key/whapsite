The Mongolian Invasion

Mongolia

You make the decision to weigh anchor and allow the Korean fleet to catch up to strengthen your forces.

However, months pass without the other fleet arriving. Fearing the worst, you assume that they will never come and begin to search for a landing location. But you cannot find a suitable landing location for your fleet because of the walls built by the Japanese prevent you from finding any sort of suitable landing. Finally, you find a patch in the wall that was hastily built and is much weaker than the rest of the wall. You may not come across a better landing location, but the weakness is heavily defended by Japanese samurai.

Do you launch a full-scale attack on the weak spot in the wall or do you continue searching for an opening?
