The Mongolian Invasion

Mongolia

You make the decision to attack at dawn.

Fearing you will not come upon a better chance than this to invade Japan, you launch your men at the wall. Your troops quickly break through and force back the Japanese soldiers, overwhelming the defenders with sheer numbers. You begin to push into the surrounding countryside from your newly established beachhead. Any resistance is quickly put down and the villagers fear the sight of your Mongol army, knowing that the samurai are powerless to stop you. City after city fall as you lead your Mongols across the country. By year’s end, you have achieved total control of Japan. Once the last city is taken, you enter your tent, sit down at the table, take out parchment, ink, and a quill.

“Your excellency, I am pleased to inform you that all Japan is now under your control…”
