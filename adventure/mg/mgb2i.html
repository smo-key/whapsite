The Mongolian Invasion

Mongolia

You decide to continue fighting and try to take over all of Japan for the Khan’s empire.

You fight on the beaches and you are eventually able to make a beachhead on Honshu before settling down for the winter. After a short winter, more troops arrive from the ever generous Kublai Khan and you quickly sweep across Honshu, killing every Japanese man and raping (and killing) every Japanese woman in your path.

You capture Hojo Tokimune, the Japanese regent. He is sent to the Khan as his slave and Kublai Khan makes him his personal foot massager, demonstrating the glory and greatness of the Mongols.
