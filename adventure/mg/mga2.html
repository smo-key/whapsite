The Mongolian Invasion

Mongolia

Suspicious of Japanese traps, you continue searching for another opening.

Many days pass and you begin to run short on supplies as your fleet scours the coastline for any opening. One week later, after several skirmishes and minor victories, your fleet is caught in a typhoon that the Japanese would later hail as the kamikaze, the divine winds sent by the gods to protect them from you and your invaders. The winds and storms make it almost impossible to control the ships, and you lose sight of almost all of the other ships. Without much food, you and the few soldiers who survived hunt for land and the other ships but to no avail. After resorting to cannibalism, you finally land on some unknown land. You search for civilization, and after finding none, realize that the island is small with no water to sustain life.

You run out of water after a few days and everyone dies.
